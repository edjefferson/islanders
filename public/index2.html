Islanders

Welcome to the island!

Since 1942, BBC Radio 4 has regularly been 'marooning' celebrity castaways on a desert island. Each one of them is allowed to take with them just eight pieces of music*, which they select and discuss on the show.

Since the 1950s everyone's also been allowed a book - not including The Complete Works of Shakespeare and the Bible (or equivalent religious text), which they get for 'free', and a luxury.*

* Or other sound recording.

* Strictly speaking, it is supposed to be inanimate and not anything that could aid an escape from the island.
